The Tale of Benjamin Bunny

Cāritās

Peter sōlus sedēbat. Tunicam nōn habēbat, calceōs nōn habēbat.

Peter sat alone. He had no jacket and no shoes.

1

Māter eum in sūdārium rubrum involvit.

His mother wrapped him in a red handkerchief.

2

Beniāmīn Cunīculus cōnsobrīnus Petrī erat. Audāx erat.

Benjamin Bunny was Peter's cousin. He was very bold.

3

Beniāmīn Petrum invēnit. "Cūr trīstis es?" rogāvit.

Benjamin found Peter. "Why are you sad?" he asked.

4

"Tunicam in hortō āmīsī," inquit Peter.

"I lost my jacket in the garden," said Peter.

5

"Venī!" inquit Beniāmīn. "Eam recipiēmus!"

"Come!" said Benjamin. "We will get it back!"

6

Mūrum hortī ascendērunt et dēspexērunt.

They climbed up the garden wall and looked down.

7

Peter dē mūrō cecidit! In hortum dēvolūtus est.

Peter fell down from the wall! He tumbled into the garden.

8

Tunicam Petrī in formīdine vīdērunt!

They saw Peter's jacket on the scarecrow!

9

Beniāmīn tunicam dētrāxit. Peter eam induit.

Benjamin took the jacket off the scarecrow. Peter put it on.

10

Tum cēpās collēgērunt. Corbulam implēvērunt.

Then they picked onions to fill their basket.

11

Beniāmīn lactūcam ēdit. Nihil timēbat.

Benjamin ate some lettuce. He was not afraid at all.

12

Peter cēpās identidem āmittēbat. Trepidābat!

Peter kept dropping the onions. He was nervous!

13

Peter sonitum audīvit. "Abīre dēbēmus!" inquit.

Peter heard a noise. "We must go!" he said.

14

Tum fēlem vīdērunt! Magna erat.

Then they saw the cat! She was very big.

15

Sub corbulā sē abdidērunt. Fēlēs suprā cōnsēdit!

They hid under a basket. The cat sat on top!

16

Diū exspectāvērunt. Sē movēre nōn poterant.

They waited and waited. They could not move.

17

Tum senex Dominus Bouncer vēnit! Pater Beniāmīnī erat.

Then old Mr. Bouncer came! He was Benjamin's father.

18

Dominus Bouncer in fēlem insiluit! Fēlēs fūgit!

Mr. Bouncer jumped on the cat! She ran away!

19

Beniāmīnum auribus prehendit. Beniāmīn poenās dabat.

He pulled Benjamin out by the ears. Benjamin was in trouble.

20

Dominus Bouncer eōs domum dūxit. Per portam exiērunt!

Mr. Bouncer marched them all home. Out the gate!

21

Peter cēpās mātrī dedit. Tunicam suam habēbat iterum!

Peter gave the onions to his mother. He had his jacket back!

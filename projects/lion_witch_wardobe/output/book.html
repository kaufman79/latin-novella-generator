Cāritās

Tempore Bellī Secundī Orbis Terrārum Petrus, Susanna, Edmundus et Lucia Pevensiē in domō Professōris habitābant.

During World War II, Peter, Susan, Edmund, and Lucy Pevensie lived in the Professor's house.

1

Diē pluviōsō Luciā in armārium intrāvit.

On a rainy day, Lucy entered the wardrobe.

2

Subitō in terram nivis cecidit.

Suddenly she fell into the snowy land.

3

Lampā lūcēns viam mōnstrābat.

A glowing lamp showed the way.

4

Faunus Tumnus Luciam ad theam invītāvit.

Faun Tumnus invited Lucy to tea.

5

Dīxit Albam Maleficam semper hiemem facere et diem Nātālem vetāre.

He said the White Witch makes eternal winter and forbids Christmas.

6

Posteā Luciam domum redīre iussit, quamquam id facere nōn licēbat.

Afterward he ordered Lucy to return home, though it was not allowed.

7

Frātrēs eī nōn crēdidērunt.

Her siblings did not believe her.

8

Mox omnēs armārium intrāvērunt et Narniam vēram invēnērunt.

Soon all entered the wardrobe and found real Narnia.

9

Edmundus clam discessit et Maleficam in currū ā rēnis tractō convenit.

Edmund sneaked away and met the Witch in her reindeer-pulled sledge.

10

Malefica Edmundō dēliciās Turcīcās dedit.

The Witch gave Edmund Turkish Delights.

11

Prōmīsit eum rēgem factūram esse, sī frātrēs trāderet.

She promised to make him king if he betrayed his siblings.

12

Aliī ad domum Castorum vēnērunt.

The others came to the Beavers' house.

13

Ibi audiērunt Aslanem, leōnem magnum, redīre.

There they heard the great lion Aslan was returning.

14

In viā Pater Nātālis venit.

On the way Father Christmas came.

15

Dēdit Petrō gladium et clipeum, Susannae arcum et sagittās, Luciae pōtiōnem sānantem et pūgiōnem.

He gave Peter a sword and shield, Susan a bow and arrows, Lucy a healing potion and dagger.

16

Maleficae Edmundī prōditiōne eum cēpit.

By Edmund's betrayal the Witch captured him.

17

Aslan aliōs ad Mēnsam Lapideam vocāvit. Malefica dīxit Edmundō mortem merērī.

Aslan called the others to the Stone Table. The Witch said Edmund deserved death.

18

Aslan sē prō eō moritūrum esse dīxit. Mediā nocte Aslan occīsus est.

Aslan said he would die for him. At midnight Aslan was killed.

19

Lucia et Susanna flēbant.

Lucy and Susan wept.

20

Aurōra venit. Magīa profundior Aslanem revocāvit.

Dawn came. Deeper magic revived Aslan.

21

Vīvit iterum! Currit ad castellum Maleficae.

He lives again! He runs to the Witch's castle.

22

Statuās lapideās tangit et vīvās facit.

He touches the stone statues and makes them live.

23

In proeliō apud flūmen Petrus fortiter pugnat.

In the battle by the river Peter fights bravely.

24

Aslan baculum Maleficae frangit et eam vincit.

Aslan breaks the Witch's wand and defeats her.

25

Nix liquēscit, et vēr venit.

Snow melts, and spring comes.

26

Aslan eōs apud Cair Paravel corōnat.

Aslan crowns them at Cair Paravel.

27

Petrus Magnificus, Susanna Lenis, Edmundus Iūstus, Luciā Valēns fīunt.

They become Peter the Magnificent, Susan the Gentle, Edmund the Just, Lucy the Valiant.

28

Post multōs annōs cervum album vident et eum sequuntur. Per armārium redeunt in Angliam, paulō postquam discesserant.

Years later they see a white stag and follow it. Through the wardrobe they return to England, shortly after they left.

29

Professor rīdet et dīcit: “Narnia iterum vōs vocābit.”

The Professor smiles and says: 'Narnia will call you again.'

30

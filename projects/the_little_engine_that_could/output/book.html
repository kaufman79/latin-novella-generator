Machina Parva Quae Potuit

The Little Engine That Could

Puf, puf, puf! Parva machina per viās ferrātās ībat.

Chug, chug, chug! A little engine went along the tracks.

1

Hamaxostichus plēnus erat rērum bonārum prō līberīs.

The train was full of good things for children.

2

Pūpae et ursulī! Māla aurantia et māla! Et lac frīgidum dulce!

Dolls and teddy bears! Oranges and apples! And cold, sweet milk!

3

Līberī trāns montem exspectābant.

The children on the other side of the mountain were waiting.

4

Sed parva machina cōnstitit! Prōgredī nōn poterat.

But the little engine stopped! She could not go on.

5

Lūdicra sollicita erant. "Quis nōs trāns montem trahet?"

The toys were worried. "Who will pull us over the mountain?"

6

Machina nova splendida praeteriit. "Nōs trahe!" clāmāvērunt lūdicra.

A shiny new engine came by. "Pull us!" cried the toys.

7

"EGŌ vōs traham?" inquit Machina Splendida. "Minimē!" Et abiit.

"I pull YOU?" said the Shiny Engine. "No!" And she rushed away.

8

Machina magna fortis praeteriit. "Nōs adiuvā!" clāmāvērunt lūdicra.

A big, strong engine came by. "Help us!" cried the toys.

9

"Rēs magnās ferō," inquit Machina Magna. "Nimis occupātus sum." Et abiit.

"I carry important things," said the Big Engine. "I am too busy." And he puffed away.

10

Machina vetusta lentē advēnit. "Nōs adiuvā! Tē quaesō!"

A tired old engine came slowly. "Help us! Please!"

11

"Nōn possum. Nōn possum," inquit Machina Vetusta. "Fessus sum." Et abiit.

"I cannot. I cannot," said the Old Engine. "I am tired." And he crept away.

12

Tum parva machina caerulea advēnit. Valdē parva erat.

Then a little blue engine came along. She was very small.

13

"Parva sum. Numquam trāns montem iī."

"I am small. I have never been over the mountain."

14

Sed līberī lūdicra dēsīderant. "Possum, putō," inquit. "POSSUM, PUTŌ."

But the children need their toys. "I think I can," she said. "I THINK I CAN."

15

Trāxit et trāxit. "Possum, putō! Possum, putō!" Sursum, sursum!

She pulled and pulled. "I can, I think! I can, I think!" Up, up!

16

"Possum, putō! Possum, putō!" Altius et altius ascendit.

"I can, I think! I can, I think!" Higher and higher she climbed.

17

Valdē fessa erat. Sed nōn cōnstitit. "POSSUM, PUTŌ!"

She was so tired. But she did not stop. "I CAN, I THINK!"

18

POSSUM! Ad summum montem pervēnit!

I CAN! She reached the top of the mountain!

19

Dē monte dēscendit. "Potuī! Potuī! Putāvī! Putāvī!"

Down the mountain she went. "I could! I could! I thought so! I thought so!"

20

Līberī accurērunt! "Lūdicra adsunt! Cibus adest!"

The children came running! "The toys are here! The food is here!"

21

Parva machina caerulea subrīsit. "Potuī," molliter inquit.

The little blue engine smiled. "I could," she said softly.

22

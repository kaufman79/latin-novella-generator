Lincus et Thēsaurus Raptus

Link and the Stolen Treasure

Lincus trāns mare obscūrum nāvigāvit.

Link sailed across the dark sea.

1

"Moblīnī thēsaurum rapuērunt," inquit Tetra. "Refer!"

"The Moblins stole the treasure," said Tetra. "Get it back."

2

Lincus sē in doliō cēlāvit. Dolium per āera volāvit!

Link hid inside a barrel. The barrel flew through the air!

3

Dolium in arcem cecidit. Lincus tacitē prōspexit.

The barrel landed in the fortress. Link peeked out quietly.

4

Lūx magna per terram sē mōvit. Lincus sē cēlāvit!

A big light moved across the ground. Link hid!

5

Lincus tacitē cucurrit. Ā doliō ad dolium rēpsit.

Link ran quietly. From barrel to barrel he crept.

6

Moblīnus sē vertit! Lincus nōn sē mōvit.

A Moblin turned around! Link did not move.

7

Moblīnus eum vīdit! "ECCE!" clāmāvit.

The Moblin saw him! "THERE!" it shouted.

8

Moblīnī ex omnī ōstiō cucurrērunt. Lincum cēpērunt!

Moblins ran from every door. They grabbed Link!

9

Moblīnī Lincum ex arce iēcērunt. Iterum per āera volāvit.

They threw Link out of the fortress. He flew through the air again.

10

Tetra Lincum ē marī trāxit. Nihil habēbat.

Tetra pulled Link from the sea. He had nothing.

11

Lincus sōlus sēdit. Arx obscūra et procul erat.

Link sat alone. The fortress was dark and far away.

12

Moblīnōs meminit. Redīre nōlēbat.

He remembered the Moblins. He did not want to go back.

13

Tetra iuxtā eum sēdit. "Iterum temptā," inquit lēniter.

Tetra sat next to him. "You can try again," she said quietly.

14

Lincus arcem spectāvit. Adhūc timēbat. Sed surrēxit.

Link looked at the fortress. He was still afraid. But he stood up.

15

Iterum sē in doliō cēlāvit. Iterum per āera volāvit!

Link hid in a barrel again. Again he flew through the air!

16

Iterum in arcem cecidit. Hāc vice diūtius exspectāvit.

Again he landed in the fortress. This time he waited longer.

17

Iterum lūx magna vēnit. Lincus dolium super sē posuit!

Again the big light came. Link put a barrel over himself!

18

Praeter Moblīnōs ambulāvit! Sōlum dolium vīdērunt.

He walked right past the Moblins! They saw only a barrel.

19

Lincus thēsaurum invēnit. Iterum arcam aperuit. Tacitē, hāc vice.

Link found the treasure. Again he opened the chest. Quietly this time.

20

Lincus tacitē ex arce rēpsit. Thēsaurum arte tenēbat.

Link crept out of the fortress. He held the treasure tight.

21

In mare dēsiluit. Hāc vice rīdēbat.

He jumped into the sea. This time he was smiling.

22

Tetra eum iterum trāxit. "Fēcistī!" inquit.

Tetra pulled him up again. "You did it!" she said.

23

Lincus timēbat. Sed iterum temptāvit. Fortis erat.

Link was afraid. But he tried again. He was brave.

24

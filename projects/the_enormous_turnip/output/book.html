The Enormous Turnip

Avus sēmen sēvit.

Grandfather planted a seed.

1

Rāpa crēvit. Crēvit et crēvit!

The turnip grew. It grew and grew!

2

Rāpa INGĒNS erat!

The turnip was ENORMOUS!

3

Avus rāpam trahit. Trahit et trahit. NŌN POTEST!

Grandfather pulled the turnip. He pulled and pulled. He COULD NOT!

4

Avus Aviam vocāvit. "Venī! Mē adiuvā!" Avia Avum prehendit. Trahunt et trahunt. NŌN POSSUNT!

"Grandmother, come help!" Grandmother grabbed Grandfather. They pulled and pulled. They COULD NOT!

5

Avia Puellam vocāvit. "Venī! Nōs adiuvā!" Puella Aviam prehendit. Trahunt et trahunt. NŌN POSSUNT!

"Granddaughter, come help!" They all pulled. They COULD NOT!

6

Puella Canem vocāvit. "Venī! Nōs adiuvā!" Canis Puellam prehendit. Trahunt et trahunt. NŌN POSSUNT!

"Dog, come help!" They all pulled. They COULD NOT!

7

Canis Fēlem vocāvit. "Venī! Nōs adiuvā!" Fēlēs Canem prehendit. Trahunt et trahunt. NŌN POSSUNT!

"Cat, come help!" They all pulled. They COULD NOT!

8

Fēlēs Mūrem vocāvit. "Venī! Nōs adiuvā!" "Sed tam parva sum!" inquit Mūs.

"Mouse, come help!" "But I am so small!" said Mouse.

9

"Venī! Nōs adiuvā!" omnēs clāmāvērunt.

"Come! Help us!" they all said.

10

Mūs caudam Fēlis prehendit. Omnēs trahunt!

Mouse grabbed Cat's tail. Everyone pulled!

11

Trahunt... et trahunt... et trahunt...

They pulled... and pulled... and pulled...

12

POSSUNT! Rāpa ē terrā exit!

THEY COULD! The turnip came out!

13

Rāpam domum omnēs portāvērunt.

They carried the turnip home together.

14

Avia iūs rāpārum fēcit. Tantum iūris!

Grandmother made turnip soup. SO MUCH soup!

15

Omnēs ūnā cēnāvērunt.

They all ate together.

16

Mūs plūrimum ēdit!

Mouse ate the most!

17

Omnēs adiūvērunt. Etiam minima.

Everyone helped. Even the smallest.

18

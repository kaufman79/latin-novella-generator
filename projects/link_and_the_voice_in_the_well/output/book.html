Link and the Voice in the Well

Lincus omnem sēmitam īnsulae sciēbat. Omne saxum. Omnem arborem.

Link knew every path on the island. Every rock. Every tree.

1

Sed hunc puteum numquam vīderat.

But he had never seen this well.

2

Dēspēxit. Obscūrum. Valdē obscūrum.

He looked down into it. Dark. Very dark.

3

Tum — vōx. Parva. Longē īnfrā.

Then — a voice. Small. Far below.

4

"Adesne adhūc?" dīxit vōx. Lincus sē inclīnāvit. "Adsum!" clāmāvit.

"Are you still there?" said the voice. Link leaned closer. "I'm here!" he called.

5

Fūnem invēnit. Dēmīsit — deorsum, deorsum, deorsum.

He found a rope. He lowered it down, down, down.

6

Nimis brevis.

Too short.

7

Vītem longam invēnit. Fūnī alligāvit. Deorsum, deorsum, deorsum.

He found a long vine. He tied it to the rope. Down, down, down.

8

Adhūc nimis brevis. "Adesne adhūc?" dīxit vōx.

Still too short. "Are you still there?" said the voice.

9

"Adsum!" clāmāvit Lincus. Cingulum dētrāxit. Alligāvit.

"I'm here!" Link called. He took off his belt. He tied it on.

10

Nimis brevis! Līneam aviae cēpit. Alligāvit.

Too short! He took Grandma's clothesline. He tied it on.

11

Deorsum, deorsum, deorsum. Nimis brevis!

Down, down, down. Too short!

12

Vōx minor erat. "Adesne adhūc?"

The voice was smaller now. "Are you still there?"

13

"Adsum," dīxit Lincus. Forāmen obscūrum spectāvit. Fūnem rapuit.

"I'm here," said Link. He looked at the dark hole. He grabbed the rope.

14

Intrāvit. Deorsum. In tenebrās.

He climbed in. Down. Into the dark.

15

Saxum ūdum. Manūs frīgidae. Fūnis dēfēcit. Pede marginem invēnit.

Wet stone. Cold hands. The rope ended. He found a ledge with his foot.

16

Tum — duo oculī. In tenebrīs lūcēbant.

Then — two eyes. Glowing in the dark.

17

Faciēs parva. Folium parvum. Foliolus!

A tiny face. A little leaf. Foliolus!

18

Lincus Foliolum in umerōs posuit. Ascendit.

Link put Foliolus on his shoulders. He climbed up.

19

Sōl! Oculōs clausērunt. "Adesne adhūc?" dīxit Foliolus. "Adsum," dīxit Lincus.

Sunlight! They blinked. "Are you still there?" said Foliolus. "I'm here," said Link.

20

Foliolus sē excussit. Folium sē vertit. Sursum volāvit! Volāre poterat!

Foliolus shook himself. His leaf spun. He floated up! He could fly!

21

Foliolus sēmen in caput Lincī dēmīsit. Tum abiit.

Foliolus dropped a seed on Link's head. Then he was gone.

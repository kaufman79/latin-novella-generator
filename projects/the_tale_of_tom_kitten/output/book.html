Trēs fēlīculī erant: Digitālia, Thomas, et Moppet.

There were three little kittens: Mittens, Tom Kitten, and Moppet.

1

Ōlim māter convīvium parāre volēbat.

One day their mother wanted to have a tea party.

2

Fēlīculōs lāvit et pīlōs eōrum dēpēxit.

She washed the kittens and brushed their fur.

3

Caudās et vibrissās eōrum pēxit.

She combed their tails and whiskers.

4

Thomam tunicā caeruleā vestīvit. Sed Thomas nimis pinguis erat!

She dressed Tom in a blue suit. But Tom was very fat!

5

Fībulae dēsiluērunt! Māter eās resūit.

Several buttons popped off. Mother sewed them back on.

6

"Īte in hortum! Vestīmenta munda servāte!" inquit Māter.

"Go into the garden. Keep your clothes clean!" said Mother.

7

Moppet et Digitālia per hortum ambulābant. In stōlīs suīs offendērunt!

Moppet and Mittens walked down the path. They tripped on their dresses!

8

In hortō cecidērunt et sordidī factī sunt.

They fell in the garden and got dirty.

9

Thomas mūrum ascendit. Petasus dēcidit! Fībulae iterum dēsiluērunt!

Tom climbed up the wall. His hat fell off! His buttons burst!

10

Moppet et Digitālia Thomam adiuvāre temptāvērunt. Sed vestīmenta omnia cecidērunt!

Moppet and Mittens tried to help Tom. But all his clothes fell off!

11

Tum trēs anatēs in viā vēnērunt. Pit pat! Pit pat!

Then three ducks came down the road. Pit pat paddle pat!

12

Anatēs petasum et vestīmenta Thomae sustulērunt!

The ducks picked up Tom's hat and clothes!

13

Fēlīculī rīsērunt! Digitālia dē mūrō cecidit!

The kittens laughed! Mittens fell off the wall!

14

Anas tunicam caeruleam Thomae induit. Nōn conveniēbat!

Mr. Drake put on Tom's blue suit. It did not fit!

15

Anatēs in vestīmentīs fēlīculōrum abiērunt.

The ducks walked away in the kittens' clothes.

16

Tum Māter in hortum vēnit. Fēlīculī nūdī erant!

Then Mother came into the garden. The kittens had no clothes on!

17

Māter īrātissima erat! Fēlīculōs pūnīvit!

Mother was very angry. She smacked the kittens!

18

Eōs in lectum mīsit. Sed fēlīculī in lectō nōn mānsērunt!

She sent them to bed. But the kittens did not stay in bed!

19

Et vestīmenta anatum in stāgnum cecidērunt!

And the ducks' clothes fell off in the pond!
